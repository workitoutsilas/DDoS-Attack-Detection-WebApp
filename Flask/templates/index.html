SIDE NOTE : AVERAGE PREDICTION PROBABILITY

The chart shows a statistics on the average prediction probabilities of our model.

To account for the possibility of misclassification, prediction probabilities are used, allowing users to see the model's level of certainty. For visualization purposes, the average prediction probabilities of both classes (malicious and normal) are computed for the uploaded traffic file.

A prediction of 0 indicates that the traffic is likely malicious, while a prediction of 1 suggests that the traffic is most likely normal. To visualize the technical results of the predictions, click on the "See Details" button.
